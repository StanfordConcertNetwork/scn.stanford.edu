Stanford Concert Network is a student organization at Stanford University dedicated to bringing live music to the Stanford bubble. We organize the annual Frost Music & Arts Festival in May as well as host frequent shows featuring rising artists of diverse styles. Our mission is to provide our student members with educational experiences within the music industry, sound tech, marketing, finances, and more.
